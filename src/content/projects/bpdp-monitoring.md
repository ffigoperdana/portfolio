## Overview

Built dashboards for virtual-machine and application availability, resource utilization, HTTP status, service health, and centralized logs across BPDP production services.

## Approach

Prometheus, Grafana, Loki, Alloy, Node Exporter, and Blackbox Exporter provide metrics, probing, and log collection. Application probes and log pipelines feed focused operational views.

## Operations

Configured Grafana alert rules and notification delivery, tuned noisy alerts, and documented onboarding and troubleshooting procedures for the operating team.
