## Overview

Plasmo is a platform for COVID-19 patients who need convalescent plasma to find prospective donors. It was built with Laravel 8 and the Stisla admin framework as a Dicoding capstone during the pandemic.

## Problem

People seeking plasma and people willing to donate needed a clearer digital path to find one another during a time-sensitive health emergency.

## Approach

The project uses Laravel, PHP, Blade, and Docker to deliver the web application. It was deployed on Heroku, with the application interface supporting the donor and patient matching flow.
